The most inspiring person in my life is Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, one such grand human being whose achievements, humility, sincerity, hard work and never give up outlook had and would always impart a great moral fillip for all at every stages of life.

He was born in a poor family in Rameshwaram. When family conditions became a villain in his life he started selling newspaper and continued his studies which shows us his utmost dedication for studies. No doubt he became the 11th president of India along with many honours.

The character of him which inspired the most is never give up outlook. When we have a never give up character it creates a positive aura around us that will make us fight till the end. Dr. Kalam was one gallant fighter since his childhood and his bravery to face challenges with a smile right as a kid made me him the best person for me. ―End is not the end in fact END mean Effort Never Dies- If you get No as an answer remember NO means Next Opportunity. life always reminds me to be humble. It is equally important to be humble as perseverance to conquer is.
