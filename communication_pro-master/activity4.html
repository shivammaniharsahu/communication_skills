ACTIVITY 4

DIALOGUE: FORMAL AND INFORMAL

  • Situational Dialogues /Role Play Objective:

    • Enables the learners to enhance spontaneous oral communication skills. To familiarize the students with the both formal and informal ways of conversation and role play situations. Enables the learners in making use of proper body language and facial expressions.

  • Advantage for learners:

    • The learners will be disseminating with the variety of techniques of speaking on various contexts.Will be acquainted with confident conversational skills.

  • Review: Introduction

    • Role playing games, exercises and activities facilitate in fabricating teams, enhances employee motivation, improve communications and essential to meet the requirement of corporate communication it can be practiced in pairs and even in groups with the primary aim of improving learning strategies with confidence and motivation.

  • Role Play

    • Role Play is a significant event of communication activity which focuses on the fastest way of improving speaking and listening skills for real life situations. Emotions and expressions are being used effectively when enacted with partners. Give information related to characters played in the specific role which plays a very important role in polishing language use and develops effective pronunciation and accent. The scope is once can talk about anything of the choice as characters enact in the play. Different types of role play

  • Situation Role Plays

    • Situation Role Plays enhance speaking English with right sentences and pronunciation. Examples: At theatre, restaurant, in the bank, placement Interview

  • Story Role Plays

    • In Story Role Plays the role play conversation is based on the story narration.

  • Short Discussions
