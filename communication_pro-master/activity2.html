  12. A WALK TO REMEMBER

    13. Walking down the streets of Munnar at 5 in the morning is one of the most satisfying things one can do. It was drizzling making the weather quite pleasant. The lush green mountains, clean roads that slightly chilled atmosphere made it much more satisfying. The scenic beauty of the place added to its serenity.

    Having walked for a while, we reached a waterfall. The chirping of regional birds, the sound of water falling on the rocks combined with the soothing sweet smell of soil refreshed the mood. Those moments were just memorable and dipping feet in the cold water was too serene. All the worries vanished and tension found no room in my head. All I could feel was calmness and peace of mind. Having morning tea in a stall by the waterfall with mountains on a side was relishing. Satisfaction is not found in the concrete jungles but in the lap of nature. To sum up, it was a treat to the soul.
