GROUP DISCUSSION

Nature of Group Discussion:

Group Discussion is an important activity in academic, business and administrative spheres. It is a systematic and purposeful interactive process. The exchange of ideas, thoughts and feelings take place through oral communication. The participant sitting each other almost in semi-circle and express their views in the given topic or problem. Group discussion can be defined as an activity in which small number of persons meet face to face and exchange and share ideas freely or attempt to reach a decision on a common issue. Group discussions are instructed and less formal compared to meetings or conferences where in specific roles are assigned to the participants. In a GD each participant is free to speak his views. A successful discussion involves both listening and speaking.

Difference between GD and Debate:

A G.D is an exchange of ideas, intimation, opinions between a group of people. Here participants take part in the discussion freely and a healthy exchange of ideas and opinions. It does not matter if a speaker in a group discussion is taking a position for or against the topic as long as he can justify his position through reasoning. There is no winning or losing in a group discussion as the process leads to a better understanding of a topic. G.D s have become an important tool for selection of right candidates for an organization as they reveal certain attributes .Many people seem to be knowledgeable, tongue tied in G.D s to screen such people G.D as help to select worthy candidates.

A debate is a formal contest of argumentation between two individuals or teams. In a debate two speakers exchange their views as a subject or several public issues. Speakers are given chance to speak as they counter the points raised by others with the help of their arguments. An audience is a part of the debate and there is no input from the audience. Debates are meant to be constructive through an exchange of ideas but usually it is seen that speakers try to score brownie points over each other as also to win over audience naming it a destructive debate. However, the basic purpose of a debate is healthy exchange of ideas and opinions.
